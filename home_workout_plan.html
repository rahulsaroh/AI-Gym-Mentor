Why this split works for fat loss

Push-Pull-Legs ensures every muscle group gets trained twice per week with adequate recovery. HIIT on Thursday (between lower and full body) is strategically placed so it doesn't interfere with leg strength sessions. Saturday's core + LISS combo burns fat without stressing the CNS before Sunday rest.

01
Progressive overload is mandatory
Increase weight by 5% or add 1–2 reps each week. If it doesn't challenge you, it doesn't change you. Fat loss needs muscle stress.
02
Rest periods matter
Strength sets: 60–75 sec rest. HIIT: as prescribed. Longer rest kills the fat-burning effect. Use a timer.
03
Protein within 30 min of training
Have eggs, paneer, or a protein shake right after every session. This is when muscle repair — and fat loss — happens fastest.
04
Track your tummy + hips weekly
These are your two problem areas. Measure every Sunday morning, same time, before eating. Tape doesn't lie — the scale does.
